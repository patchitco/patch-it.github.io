What is Patch It?

Patch It! is a product designed to cover up the holes in your shoes. Originally meant for skateboarders who have to deal with holes in their kicks, anyone can use the product to quickly fix holes or simply to customise their outfits.

The patches listed below will come in "kits." Each kit has three two-by-two inch patches, a small spatula for applying the adhesive, and the adhesive itself.
